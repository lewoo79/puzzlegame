Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 7, 2026

Block Journey is a mobile puzzle game developed by LEWOO / Levent Mutlu.

This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles user information.

1. Information We Collect

Block Journey does not collect, store, sell, or share any personal information from users.

The app does not require account registration and does not ask for personal details such as name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or payment information.

2. Permissions

Block Journey may use the device vibration feature to provide gameplay feedback during the game.

The vibration permission is used only for in-game effects and does not collect any personal data.

3. Internet and Third-Party Services

The current version of Block Journey does not use login systems, analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking services.

If future versions include ads, analytics, or online features, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

4. Children’s Privacy

Block Journey is a family-friendly puzzle game. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, transfer, or share user data with third parties.

6. Data Security

Because the app does not collect personal data, no personal user data is stored on our servers.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.
